About

MICA is a petrographic laboratory and software company, building advanced AI-powered tools for the Canadian mining sector. We use Multimodal Imaging to automate the Classification and Analysis of mineral grains in thin-section and core samples, allowing our clients to explore further and get results faster than traditional laboratory setups.
